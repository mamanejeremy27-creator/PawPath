Foundation

The Sit Drill

  • 1
    Hold a treat right at your dog's nose level.
  • 2
    Slowly move the treat up and back over their head.
  • 3
    As they look up, their bottom will naturally drop.
  • 4
    Mark "YES!" and reward the moment they sit.
Impulse Control

The Stay Drill

  • 1
    Ask your dog to sit and hold your palm out.
  • 2
    Say "Stay" and take one small step back.
  • 3
    If they stay, return immediately to their side.
  • 4
    Mark "YES!" and reward for holding position.
Utility

Hand Targeting

  • 1
    Present your flat palm a few inches away.
  • 2
    Wait for your dog to sniff or boop your hand.
  • 3
    The moment their nose touches skin, mark "YES!"
  • 4
    Reward from your OTHER hand.
Focus

Eye Contact

  • 1
    Hold a treat to your dog's nose to get interest.
  • 2
    Slowly bring the hand up towards your eyes.
  • 3
    The moment they make eye contact, mark "YES!"
  • 4
    Reward generously to build focus.
